Service

We deliver our best to help customers to create their perfect tailored suits.

Q:How to order a suit with us?

  1. Make an appointment (2-3 days before your visit at our stores)
  2. Select Style
  3. Choose Fabric
  4. Get measured
  5. First-time Fitting(After 10-days hand-made process)
  6. Adjustments & Fittings
  7. Enjoy your suits!

Q:Can I order online instead?

Not now. We are considering expanding the business online, but for now we only serve customers at stores with advanced appointments. Also speaking of a tailored suit, we need to have your perfect measurement and have you tried on the suits multiple times before we deliver the finished products to you. However,you can browse our available looks from the "Lookbook" and "Events" pages and start imaging your customized suits before meeting with us.

If you have more concerns, please contact us!
