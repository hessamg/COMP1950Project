Eddie's Story

Eddie is a hand-made tailored suit business located at Lan Kwai Fang in HongKong. We believe that a chic customized suit reflects one's unique identity as well as we value unique and high-quality design.

With our love for haute couture and men's clothing, we dedicate our passion to creating tailored suits for a variety of customers,becoming a noticeable fashion-brand in Hongkong's tailoring and fashion industries. We started our first store in HongKong, where suits are highly demanded by the local and international businessman and fashion lovers.

As our founder Eddie not only views himself as a brand-designer, but also a trend-maker that he hopes to share his love for suits with customers in mainland China.

sAfter two-year preparation, we opened up our second store at in Guangzhou city. Eddie's hopes to bring this exciting trend to this city that suits can become an essential of men's daily working and social life.






Our Founder-Eddie

Graduated from Hong Kong Polytechnic University’s Finance and Accounting faculty, Eddie started working at a finance agency in HongKong's central district. Calling by his enthusiasm for fashion, he decided to pursue a career in fashion by giving up his job in Finance.

Later Eddie experienced in working at Penhaligon's perfume and Esquire magazine respectively. When an opportunity came in 2014, Eddie started his own business in Tailored suit in Hongkong with gusto.On the side of his business, he returned to Hong Kong Polytechnic University’s studying Fashion Design to advance his product’s development.
